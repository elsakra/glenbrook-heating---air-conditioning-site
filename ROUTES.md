# Generated Routes

This document lists all the routes created for the Glenbrook Heating & Air Conditioning website.

## Main Pages

- `/` - Homepage (src/pages/index.astro)
- `/services` - Services overview page (src/pages/services.astro)
- `/about` - About us page (src/pages/about.astro)
- `/contact` - Contact page with form (src/pages/contact.astro)
- `/blog` - Blog index page (src/pages/blog/index.astro)
- `/blog/[slug]` - Individual blog post pages (src/pages/blog/[slug].astro)

## Legal Pages

- `/privacy` - Privacy policy (src/pages/privacy.astro)
- `/terms` - Terms of service (src/pages/terms.astro)

## Blog Posts

- `/blog/hvac-maintenance-tips` - Essential HVAC Maintenance Tips for Homeowners
- `/blog/energy-efficient-heating` - How to Make Your Home Heating More Energy Efficient
- `/blog/choosing-right-hvac-system` - Choosing the Right HVAC System for Your Home

## Notes

- All routes are functional and link to real pages
- No original site navigation was provided, so routes were created based on standard HVAC business needs
- Blog functionality implemented using Astro Content Collections
- All internal links in header/footer point to actual routes
- External links (phone, email) are preserved as provided
- Logo links to homepage as expected
- Mobile-responsive navigation included
